About Primavera Companion

Primavera Companion is a machine learning project that helps music fans make the most of their Primavera Sound festival experience by generating personalized artist recommendations based on their music taste.

The Problem

Music festivals like Primavera Sound feature hundreds of artists across multiple days and stages. It's impossible to see everyone, and sometimes the most enjoyable performances come from artists you didn't know before. How do you decide which artists to prioritize?

The Solution

Primavera Companion analyzes your personal music preferences through your Spotify playlist data and compares them to the artists performing at Primavera Sound. Using machine learning algorithms, it identifies which festival artists best match your taste, even if you've never heard them before.

Technology Behind It

Data Processing

Pandas and NumPy for data manipulation and preparation

Machine Learning

Scikit-learn for regression models that predict your affinity for artists

Web Application

Flask for the web framework, with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for the frontend

Visualization

Matplotlib for creating visual representations of recommendations

How It Works - The Technical Details

  1. Data Collection

    You export your Spotify playlist data using Exportify, which includes audio features like tempo, energy, danceability, and genre information.

  2. Data Processing

    The system processes both your playlist and the Primavera Sound lineup, handling artist collaborations and aggregating features at the artist level.

  3. Feature Engineering

    We identify shared genres between your taste and festival artists, and create numerical features representing musical characteristics.

  4. Model Training

    The system trains multiple regression models (Ridge, Lasso, Random Forest, Gradient Boosting, and SVR) to predict which Primavera artists you would add to your playlist.

  5. Artist Ranking

    Using the best-performing model, the system ranks all Primavera artists based on their predicted affinity score, with a small boost for artists already in your playlist.

  6. Visualization & Results

    The results are presented as an interactive list and data visualization, which you can download for offline use.
